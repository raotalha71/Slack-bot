---

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Northgate Sporting Goods operates five retail locations and a Shopify storefront that together generate approximately $14M in annual revenue. Inventory is currently managed in a legacy retail system (Lightspeed) that does not sync with Shopify in real time. Staff at each store location manage their own inventory records with limited visibility into what other stores hold, and the online store frequently oversells items that are out of stock or allocated to in-store holds.

The operations team spends approximately 15 hours per week reconciling inventory discrepancies between channels, investigating customer complaints about orders that could not be fulfilled, and manually updating stock levels across systems.

This proposal outlines a unified inventory management layer that synchronises stock across all five stores and the online channel in real time, provides the operations team with a single consolidated view of inventory, and automates the weekly reconciliation process.

---

PROPOSED SOLUTION

A central inventory database acts as the single source of truth for all stock levels. When a sale occurs in any channel — Lightspeed at any store, or Shopify online — the central database is updated within 30 seconds and all other channels reflect the new availability.

An operations dashboard provides real-time stock visibility across all locations and the online store. Low stock alerts notify the purchasing team when any SKU falls below a configurable threshold. A transfer recommendation engine suggests inter-store stock transfers when one location is overstocked and another is running low on the same item.

The weekly reconciliation process is replaced by a daily automated discrepancy report delivered to the operations manager each morning.

---

TECHNICAL APPROACH

- Central inventory database: PostgreSQL
- Lightspeed integration: Lightspeed Retail API (webhook-based)
- Shopify integration: Shopify Admin API (webhook-based)
- Real-time sync latency target: under 60 seconds
- Dashboard: web-based, no installation required
- Daily report: automated email via SendGrid
